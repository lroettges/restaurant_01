About

History

Coffee Cartel was founded in 1996 and is owned by Denis Gorg. We are located at the corner of Maryland Plaza and Euclid Avenue in the historic Central West End. This neighborhood is a hot spot for people of all ages seeking out St. Louis style events and culture or searching for eclectic food options and a unique ambiance. The neighborhood features victorian homes alongside european style cafes, shops, and restaurants. We find ourselves fulfilling many rolls in this part of town as a cafe, coffee shop, ice cream strore, and tea palace!

Philanthropy

We are a locally owned and run business hence our motto: Coffee Cartel - its a St. Louis Thing! We strive to support other local businesses and the community that continually supports us. In effort to support a wide variety of charity organizations and to give back to the St. Louis Community, we have hosted fundraisers, donated to, and sponsored over 290 groups in 2014 alone. If you are seeking our help please contact us at http://charity@thecoffeecartel.com/. We request that you do not call the store for this purpose.

General & Awards

The Coffee Cartel is open 24 hour per day, 7 days a week, and we never close. We are open every holiday thanks to the dedicated work of our team members. Cartel provides free wireless internet, free high speed plug ins. With 240 seats and a large patio, there’s most always a place to socialize and capture the atmosphere of the neighborhood and the people here.
